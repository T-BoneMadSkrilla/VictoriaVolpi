About Me

With experience as a Registered Nurse, I've developed expertise across multiple specialties including critical care, emergency medicine, and medical-surgical nursing. My passion for nursing and attention to detail led me to combine my clinical knowledge and passion for advocacy, becoming a Legal Nurse Consultant.

I now dedicate my career to serving both the legal community and fellow nurses. As a Legal Nurse Consultant, I provide specialized medical insight for attorneys handling healthcare-related cases. Additionally, I'm committed to supporting new nurses through comprehensive guides and resources that help bridge the gap between nursing education and clinical practice.

My mission is to elevate healthcare standards through legal advocacy and education, ensuring both patients and nurses receive the support they deserve.
