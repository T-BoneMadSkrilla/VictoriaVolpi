What they don't always tell you is that there's a huge gap between classroom theory and bedside reality. Even with orientation, that transition from student to new grad can be jarring. Suddenly you're expected to apply everything you've learned in real time, with real consequences. I started this because too many students and new nurses feel alone, confused, or afraid to ask questions. We've all been there. I never thought I was smart enough to be a nurse, but turns out, I am. I just needed the right support.

Whether you're drowning in nursing school, feeling like an imposter during your first months on the job, or just curious about healthcare... I've got your back. From mastering fluid calculations to navigating your first code blue, we'll figure this out together.
