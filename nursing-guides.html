The journey from nursing student to confident practitioner is filled with challenges that aren't always addressed in traditional education. Throughout my years as a registered nurse, I've witnessed countless new graduates struggle with the gap between classroom theory and bedside reality.

These guides are born from my passion for nursing education and mentorship. I believe that by sharing practical knowledge, clinical insights, and hard-won wisdom, we can better prepare the next generation of nurses to provide exceptional patient care while maintaining their own wellbeing.

"I create these guides because I remember what it felt like to be overwhelmed as a new nurse. Having a trusted resource to turn to can make all the difference between struggling and thriving in those crucial first years of practice."

— Victoria Volpi, RN

Each guide is meticulously crafted to address the real challenges nurses face daily, from mastering clinical skills to navigating workplace dynamics. My goal is to provide you with practical, evidence-based resources that build confidence and competence throughout your nursing journey.
