Legal Nurse Consulting

Bridging the gap between healthcare and legal practice with specialized medical expertise for attorneys handling healthcare-related cases.

Services Offered

Medical Record Review

Comprehensive analysis of medical records to identify key clinical issues, timeline inconsistencies, and standards of care concerns. I translate complex medical terminology and processes into clear language for legal professionals.

Case Merit Evaluation

Expert assessment of the strengths and weaknesses of healthcare-related cases from a clinical perspective. I help attorneys determine if there is sufficient evidence of deviation from standards of care to pursue litigation.

Chronology Development

Creation of detailed medical chronologies that organize complex healthcare events into clear, logical timelines, highlighting critical incidents and interventions relevant to the case.
